# Platform Publishing Pack

Use this reference when producing the social-platform copy that accompanies a finished video.

## Sources To Recheck

Platform rules change. When the user asks for current policy interpretation, browse official or primary sources first. Prefer:

- Douyin rules/creator center and official announcements: `douyin.com/rule`, `creator.douyin.com`
- WeChat Channels and WeChat public platform rules: `weixin.qq.com`, `channels.weixin.qq.com`, `mp.weixin.qq.com`
- Xiaohongshu official agreements/rules: `agree.xiaohongshu.com`
- China AIGC labeling and generative-AI regulations: `cac.gov.cn`

Record the review date when policy-sensitive advice is included.

## Required Sections

For each finished video, provide a Markdown publishing pack with:

- Asset list: video, cover, preview, narration text, source repo URL, Star count/capture date
- Global positioning: one-sentence account positioning and content boundary
- Platform sections for Douyin, WeChat Channels, and Xiaohongshu
- For each platform: title options, recommended title, body copy, hashtags/topics, pinned comment, AI-assisted disclosure, source/date note, risk notes
- Cross-platform high-risk wording checklist

## Platform Guidance

Douyin:

- Lead with a concrete pain or contradiction in the first line.
- Keep body copy short and scannable.
- Use a clear follow/comment CTA.
- Include AI-assisted disclosure when the video uses AI narration, generated visuals, voice cloning, or synthetic characters.
- Avoid wording that implies platform manipulation, guaranteed earnings, academic cheating, medical/legal/financial certainty, or bypassing review.

WeChat Channels:

- Use slightly calmer, trust-building copy.
- Mention source and capture date when discussing GitHub Stars.
- Encourage comments, follows, and forwarding rather than aggressive conversion.
- Avoid unsupported authority claims such as official endorsement, guaranteed outcome, or certified effectiveness.

Xiaohongshu:

- Use searchable practical titles, not pure clickbait.
- Make the body feel like a useful note: what it is, who it helps, what it cannot do.
- Add collection/comment CTA.
- Avoid exaggerated “种草” claims, absolute superlatives, fake personal experience, and unsupported before/after results.

## High-Risk Terms

Rewrite or remove:

- Academic misconduct: `代写论文`, `保过`, `包过`, `一键毕业`, `导师看不出来`
- Platform evasion: `绕过审核`, `规避风控`, `不封号`, `躲检测`
- Illegal/copyright: `破解`, `盗版`, `搬运`, `洗稿`, `无授权素材`
- Unverified guarantee: `100%`, `稳赚`, `无风险`, `官方推荐`, `全网第一`, `永久有效`
- Sensitive-domain certainty: `诊断`, `法律结论`, `投资建议`, `保收益`

Prefer:

- `流程管理`, `证据约束`, `辅助创作`, `开源项目案例`, `制作时截图数据`, `不替代专业判断`, `不是代写/不是终稿`

## Default Disclosure

Use or adapt:

`本视频为 AI 辅助创作，含 AI 配音/动画包装；项目数据以制作时公开页面截图为准，不构成官方背书。`
